Memory Recall

"Mental Recall" is a game that challenges your memory. The core objective is simple: navigate a path to reach the goal. However, the path is randomly generated, and the game's difficulty increases each time you succeed. If you slip up once, you will have to start all over again.

Created Winter 2023

Heavenly Prayers

Welcome to our app that provides personalized prayers based on your input. Begin by expressing thoughts within a 100-character space. After a brief ad, receive a unique prayer tailored to your feelings. These aren't saved, so capture meaningful ones. Our aim is to be a digital refuge, offering solace and guidance during challenges. We value your journey with us.

Created Summer 2023
